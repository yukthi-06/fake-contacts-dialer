## Verification Steps
1. Tap **Call Back** from the main menu.
2. Select a contact (e.g. David Smith) and specify a delay (e.g. `5` seconds).
3. Tap **Schedule Incoming Call**.
4. The setup activity closes. Exit to the launcher / background.
5. After 5 seconds, the incoming call screen displays, playing the ringtone.
6. Accept -> enters call and starts audio. Decline -> stops call and finishes.

## Verification Results

### Manual verification steps:
1. Open the menu in the top-right corner of the main screen and tap **Settings**.
2. Tap **Ringtone Audio File** to open the system file picker and select an audio file.
3. Tap **Play Preview** to listen to the ringtone, and **Stop Preview** to halt it.
4. Tap **Reset** to restore the default system ringtone.
5. Tap on any contact on the main screen to initiate a call; you should hear your custom ringtone playing while it shows "Calling...". Once connected or canceled, the ringtone stops.

## Verification Steps
1. Tap **Call Back** and schedule an incoming call with a 5-second delay.
2. Exit the app or wait. When the incoming call screen appears, tap **Answer**.
3. Once the active call screen appears, verify the small square camera preview shows in the bottom-left corner and video recording is active.
4. End the call. Verify a new recorded video file has been saved under `/sdcard/Vypeensoft/Contacts_Phone_Dialer/recordings/`.
